About LifeLike

What is LifeLike?

LifeLike is a social media platform built within the Cyclosphere ecosystem. Unlike traditional social networks, LifeLike prioritizes live interactions, real-time engagement, and seamless connectivity without the clutter of standard social feeds.

How LifeLike Operates

  • Live-Only – No pre-recorded posts; everything happens in real-time.
  • Interactive Streams – Go live instantly and engage with friends and followers.
  • No Privacy – Ensuring lifelike and real interactions.
  • AI-Powered Content Moderation – Smart filtering ensures a safe and effective environment.

Key Features

  • Hot mic functionality dare to be unaware
  • Record what you see download for keeps
  • Integrated live polls and Q&A sessions
  • Live chats
  • Friends and followers system
  • Direct messaging (DMs)
  • Forums for community discussions
  • User profiles with customization
  • Ability to create posts
  • Buy and sell marketplace functionality

Future Developments

LifeLike is continuously evolving to provide the best real-time social experience. Upcoming features include full VR integration, AI-generated content recommendations, and decentralized security solutions.
